Why This Matters

Many businesses look profitable on paper but still feel financially tight. That's because cash and profit don't always move in sync — delayed payments, surprise expenses, and mis-timed investments can leave you short even when sales are up.

A fractional CFO helps anticipate cash shortages and align financial resources with your growth plans, giving you a clear roadmap before problems arise.
